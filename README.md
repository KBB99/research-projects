## Core Documents

- **[The Universe as Computation](The_Universe_as_Computation.md)**  
  A beginner-friendly introduction to a unified lens on mind, language, ethics, and reality. It presents computation as an irreversible, meaning-preserving process and introduces the RSVP framework in an accessible way.

- **[A Unified Framework for Cognition, Computation, and Reality](Unified_Framework.md)**  
  A formal synthesis of the Relativistic Scalar-Vector Plenum (RSVP) and event-historical computational models. This document serves as the conceptual backbone of the research program, unifying physics, cognition, social systems, and AI alignment.

- **[Beyond the Transformer](Beyond_the_Transformer.md)**  
  A technical and philosophical critique of transformer architectures, outlining a vision for sparse, recursive, and categorical AI grounded in thermodynamics, biology, and irreversible computation.

## Historical and Interpretive Essays

- **[The Universe in a Mind: Giordano Bruno and the Ideas That Shaped a New World](Giordano_Bruno.md)**  
  A historical essay tracing the philosophical roots of field-based, infinite-world thinking through Giordano Bruno and Neo-Platonism, providing intellectual context for modern unified frameworks.

- **[I Read a Futurist’s Secret Compendium](Secret_Compendium.md)**  
  An interpretive and reflective essay distilling key ideas from a dense body of futurist theory into five conceptually disruptive insights about cognition, intelligence, progress, and reality.
