# Logic Overview: From Flickering Ideas To Permanent Worlds

## 1. The Conceptual Leap: From Stochastic Projection To Persistence

Early generative systems operate as **stochastic projections**. Each observation is effectively a new sample, with no obligation to remain consistent with prior outputs. This produces the familiar instability:

- geometry shifts  
- identities drift  
- continuity breaks  

These systems lack a **write operator**, so nothing is retained beyond the moment of observation.

The RSVP–Polyxan framework replaces this with **persistent reality**. The key transition is from:

- one-way generation  
- to bidirectional field evolution  

Once a detail is observed, it is no longer provisional. It becomes part of the system’s internal state through **causal residue**.
