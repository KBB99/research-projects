\part{Formal Verification and Proof Architecture}

Part~IV elevates the RSVP--Polyxan hyperstructure into a verifiable,
logically coherent system. These chapters introduce a complete spectrum
of formal methods: operational and denotational semantics, type theory,
modal logics, Hoare logics for embedding flows, dependent types for
hypergraph atoms, and categorical semantics for reset operators and
coherence-preserving rewrites.

The objective is to ensure that semantic galaxies are not merely elegant
simulations, but fully verified constructions with provable invariants.
RSVP PDEs, Polyxan rewrites, sheaf coherence constraints, curvature
penalties, and entropic flows are all embedded in frameworks compatible
with Lean, Coq, Agda, and other modern proof systems.

Part~IV concludes with the soundness and completeness theory for the
unified system, and a blueprint for machine-checked verification that
spans the entire RSVP--Polyxan architecture.
