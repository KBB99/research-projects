\chapter{The Unified Benchmark for Understanding}
\label{ch:unified-benchmark}

The preceding chapters established three foundational measures of epistemic
structure:

\begin{itemize}
\item the Semantic Assembly Index $\mathrm{SAI}$ (Chapter~96),
\item the KL Divergence of epistemic curvature distributions (Chapter~97),
\item the Harmonic Intelligibility Score $\mathrm{HIS}$ (Chapter~98),
\item and the epistemic curvature norm $\|\cdot\|_{\mathrm{ep}}$ unifying them (Chapter~98).
\end{itemize}

This chapter synthesises these results into a single,
verifiable, civilization-scale metric of understanding:
\[
\mathcal{U}(X)
  \;:=\;
  \frac{1}{1 + \|X\|_{\mathrm{ep}}}.
\]

This scalar lies in $(0,1]$,
and is the unique canonical benchmark of epistemic mastery
in the verified RSVP--Polyxan universe.

\section{Definition of the Unified Benchmark}

Let $X$ be any meaning-bearing structure:
an individual mind, a scientific theory, a hypergraph of beliefs,
a media quine, a semantic galaxy, or an entire civilization.

\begin{definition}[Unified Benchmark for Understanding]
\[
\mathcal{U}(X)
  :=
  \frac{1}{1 + 
    \sqrt{
      \mathrm{SAI}(X)^2
      +
      D_{\mathrm{KL}}(X\|\mathfrak{U})
      +
      \mathrm{HIS}(X)
    }
  }.
\]
\end{definition}

This benchmark has the following properties:

\begin{itemize}
\item $\mathcal{U}(X)=1$ if and only if $X\simeq\mathfrak{U}$,
\item $\mathcal{U}(X)$ approaches 0 as epistemic curvature diverges,
\item $\mathcal{U}$ decreases under any epistemic disturbance,
\item $\mathcal{U}$ increases under field descent, embedding flow, 
      reconciliation, or verification.
\end{itemize}

Understanding is therefore a curvature-normalised quantity:
a scalar measure of how near any structure stands to the epistemic vacuum.

\section{The Benchmark Decomposes Along Three Axes}

The epistemic curvature norm separates into three orthogonal components:

\[
\|X\|_{\mathrm{ep}}^2
  =
  \underbrace{\mathrm{SAI}(X)^2}_{\text{structural depth}}
  \;+\;
  \underbrace{D_{\mathrm{KL}}(X\|\mathfrak{U})}_{\text{epistemic distance}}
  \;+\;
  \underbrace{\mathrm{HIS}(X)}_{\text{intelligibility}}.
\]

Thus $\mathcal{U}(X)$ decomposes into:

\begin{description}
\item[Structural depth:]
The number of quine layers needed to assemble $X$
from primitive components (Chapter~96).

\item[Epistemic distance:]
The curvature + entropy mismatch relative to the universal quine
(Chapter~97).

\item[Intelligibility:]
The harmonic component, measuring whether $X$ can be rendered
in an epistemically transparent form (Chapter~98).
\end{description}

Each axis corresponds to a fundamental epistemic virtue:
\emph{simplicity}, \emph{alignment}, and \emph{intelligibility}.

\section{The Benchmark is the Unique Fixed-Point Invariant}

Let $\mathscr{D}$ denote any admissible epistemic transformation:
RSVP flow, EHR rewriting, reconciliation patching,
verification, or renormalization.

\begin{theorem}[Fixed-Point Invariance]
\[
\mathscr{D}(X)=X
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathcal{U}(X)=1.
\]
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
If $\mathscr{D}(X)=X$, then all curvature, entropy,
and quine-stability mismatches vanish under $\mathscr{D}$
(Chapters~13, 22, 24, 31--36).
Thus $\|X\|_{\mathrm{ep}}=0$, hence $\mathcal{U}(X)=1$.

Conversely, if $\mathcal{U}(X)=1$, then $\|X\|_{\mathrm{ep}}=0$,
so $X\simeq\mathfrak{U}$ by Chapter~98.  
All admissible transformations fix $\mathfrak{U}$.
\end{proof}

This proves that $\mathcal{U}$ singles out the unique fixed point of the epistemic universe.

\section{Irreversibility and Monotonic Convergence}

\begin{theorem}[Monotonicity of $\mathcal{U}$]
Every trajectory $X_t$ under any admissible evolution satisfies
\[
\frac{d}{dt}\mathcal{U}(X_t) > 0
\quad\text{unless}\quad
X_t\simeq\mathfrak{U}.
\]
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
Direct from
\[
\mathcal{U}(X_t)
  = \frac{1}{1+\|X_t\|_{\mathrm{ep}}}
\]
and the strict decrease of $\|X_t\|_{\mathrm{ep}}$
(Chapter~98).
\end{proof}

This is the final, scalar form of the Grand Irreversibility Theorem.
