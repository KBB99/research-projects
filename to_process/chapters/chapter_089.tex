\section{Curvature Firewalls}

If a patch $a$ violates the harmonicity threshold,
it never propagates to any other persona.
It is not reviewed.
It is not punished.
It is not flagged.

It is simply blocked by curvature.

\begin{definition}[Curvature Firewall]
For a persona $\mathfrak{q}$,
the \emph{firewall region} is:
\[
\mathcal{F}_{\mathfrak{q}}
=
\{\, a \mid \mathrm{curv}(a \otimes \mathfrak{q}) > \rho_{\max} \,\}.
\]
\end{definition}

\begin{invariant}[Firewall Isolation]
If $a \in \mathcal{F}_{\mathfrak{q}}$, then
\[
\mathfrak{p} \not\to \mathfrak{q}
\quad\text{for content }a.
\]
\end{invariant}

There is no pathway for unsafe content to travel.
The algebraic normalisation fails,
and therefore the message cannot reach its target.

Nothing is deleted.  
Nothing is suppressed.  
Nothing is judged.  
It simply cannot propagate.
