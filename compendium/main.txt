Abstract
We present a practical architecture for a Retrieval-Augmented-Generation (RAG) memory
system composed of five personas: Archivist, Formalist, Synthesist, Strategist, and Curator.
Each persona optimizes a distinct objective and is coordinated by a curator that routes memories
and enforces lifecycle policies.
