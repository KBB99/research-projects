The Relativistic Scalar-Vector Plenum (RSVP) framework reconceives longevity

waste into regenerative flow, operationalizing sustainability as entropic coherence.
Philosophically and ethically, longevity is reframed as the management of an en-
tropic budget: the freedom to transform without decohering one's recursive self.
Civilizations, organisms, and minds alike persist through recursive hepatic oper-
ators that metabolize disorder into structure, aligning personal agency with re-
versibility and systemic integrity.

but by falling outward through perpetual repair—a dynamic equilibrium in which

governs advection and coupling between regions of differing capacity, linking micro-
